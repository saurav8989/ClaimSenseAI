========================================================================
                  CLAIMSENSE AI - CLINICAL PROTOCOLS
========================================================================

This document acts as the definitive source of truth for the clinical rules
and Standard Treatment Protocols (STPs) implemented in ClaimSense AI.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. MEDICAL CODING & TERMINOLOGY DICTIONARY
------------------------------------------------------------------------

The system will recognize and support the following mappings:

*   DIAGNOSES (ICD-10-CM)
    - [B54]   Unspecified malaria (Malaria)
    - [I10]   Essential (primary) hypertension (Hypertension)
    - [E11]   Type 2 diabetes mellitus (Type 2 Diabetes)
    - [K35.8] Acute appendicitis, other and unspecified (Acute Appendicitis)

*   DIAGNOSTIC TESTS (Mock Codes)
    - [TEST-MAL-RDT]  Malaria Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT)
    - [TEST-MAL-SMEAR] Malaria Blood Smear
    - [TEST-BP-CHECK]  Blood Pressure Monitoring
    - [TEST-GLU-FPG]   Fasting Plasma Glucose (FPG)
    - [TEST-GLU-HBA1C] Glycated Hemoglobin (HbA1c)
    - [TEST-IMG-US]    Abdominal Ultrasound
    - [TEST-IMG-CT]    Abdominal CT Scan

*   TREATMENTS & PROCEDURES (Mock CPT / RxNorm Codes)
    - [MED-AL-ACT]    Artemether-Lumefantrine (ACT) (Anti-malarial)
    - [MED-AML-5]     Amlodipine 5mg (Anti-hypertensive)
    - [MED-LIS-10]    Lisinopril 10mg (Anti-hypertensive)
    - [MED-MET-500]   Metformin 500mg (Anti-diabetic)
    - [MED-INS-GLAR]  Insulin Glargine (Anti-diabetic)
    - [PROC-APPEND]   Appendectomy (Procedure) (CPT: 44950)

------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. STANDARD TREATMENT PROTOCOL (STP) RULES
------------------------------------------------------------------------

Each claim's chronological `carePathway` must follow these rules:

---

### PROTOCOL A: Malaria Uncomplicated Protocol
*   **Target Diagnosis**: B54 (Malaria)
*   **Rule 1: Diagnostic Before Treatment (Mandatory)**
    - *Condition*: If `MED-AL-ACT` is prescribed, a prior step in the care
      pathway must be a diagnostic test (`TEST-MAL-RDT` or `TEST-MAL-SMEAR`)
      with a positive result.
    - *Deviation*: Missing mandatory diagnostic test.
    - *Severity*: CRITICAL (Penalty: 40 points)
*   **Rule 2: Drug Matching (Mandatory)**
    - *Condition*: If the primary diagnosis is `B54`, the allowed therapeutics
      are strictly `MED-AL-ACT`. Prescribing other non-relevant therapies (e.g.
      antibiotics or anti-hypertensives) as a primary therapeutic is flagged.
    - *Deviation*: Unrelated medication billed for malaria.
    - *Severity*: MAJOR (Penalty: 20 points)

---

### PROTOCOL B: Essential Hypertension Protocol
*   **Target Diagnosis**: I10 (Hypertension)
*   **Rule 1: BP Check Confirmation (Mandatory)**
    - *Condition*: A claim for primary diagnosis `I10` must contain a
      `TEST-BP-CHECK` step with a recorded reading (e.g., Systolic > 140 or
      Diastolic > 90).
    - *Deviation*: Prescribing anti-hypertensive without BP record.
    - *Severity*: CRITICAL (Penalty: 40 points)
*   **Rule 2: Therapy Selection**
    - *Condition*: Allowed medications under primary diagnosis `I10` are
      restricted to `MED-AML-5` or `MED-LIS-10`.
    - *Deviation*: Non-approved medication for hypertension.
    - *Severity*: MAJOR (Penalty: 20 points)

---

### PROTOCOL C: Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus Protocol
*   **Target Diagnosis**: E11 (Type 2 Diabetes)
*   **Rule 1: Glycemic Testing (Mandatory)**
    - *Condition*: Prescribing anti-diabetic medications (`MED-MET-500` or
      `MED-INS-GLAR`) requires a documented diagnostic test (`TEST-GLU-FPG` 
      or `TEST-GLU-HBA1C`) performed within the care pathway.
    - *Deviation*: Missing glycemic diagnostic verification.
    - *Severity*: CRITICAL (Penalty: 40 points)
*   **Rule 2: Line of Treatment Sequencing**
    - *Condition*: Insulin therapy (`MED-INS-GLAR`) should only be prescribed
      if there is a documented prior trial of oral medication (`MED-MET-500`)
      OR if the patient's HbA1c result is documented as > 10%.
    - *Deviation*: Metformin trial bypassed without clinical justification.
    - *Severity*: MAJOR (Penalty: 25 points)

---

### PROTOCOL D: Acute Appendicitis Protocol
*   **Target Diagnosis**: K35.8 (Acute Appendicitis)
*   **Rule 1: Imaging Confirmation (Mandatory)**
    - *Condition*: Performing an appendectomy (`PROC-APPEND`) requires a prior
      confirmatory imaging study (`TEST-IMG-US` or `TEST-IMG-CT`) showing signs
      of appendiceal inflammation.
    - *Deviation*: Appendectomy performed without supporting ultrasound/CT.
    - *Severity*: CRITICAL (Penalty: 45 points)
*   **Rule 2: Appropriate Procedure Match**
    - *Condition*: If the primary diagnosis is `K35.8`, the claim billing list
      must include CPT code `44950` (`PROC-APPEND`).
    - *Deviation*: Mismatched billing code for acute appendicitis (e.g. no
      procedure billed, or incorrect procedure like cholecystectomy).
    - *Severity*: CRITICAL (Penalty: 40 points)

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. SCORING ENGINE LOGIC (For Developer 2)
------------------------------------------------------------------------

*   **Initial Legitimacy Score**: 100 points.
*   **Score Deductions**: Deduct points dynamically based on the penalty value
    associated with each triggered deviation.
*   **Risk Categorization Rules**:
    - **Score 80 - 100**: LOW RISK (Priority 5) -> Green Flag. Auto-approvable.
    - **Score 50 - 79**: MEDIUM RISK (Priority 3) -> Yellow Flag. Needs reviewer
      eyes.
    - **Score 0 - 49**: HIGH RISK (Priority 1) -> Red Flag. Sent to top of queue.
