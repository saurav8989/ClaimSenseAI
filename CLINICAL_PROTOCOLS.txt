========================================================================
                  CLAIMSENSE AI - CLINICAL PROTOCOLS

This document acts as the definitive source of truth for the clinical rules
and Standard Treatment Protocols (STPs) implemented in ClaimSense AI.

1. MEDICAL CODING & TERMINOLOGY DICTIONARY
------------------------------------------------------------------------

The system will recognize and support the following mappings:

*   DIAGNOSES (ICD-10-CM)
    - [B54]     Unspecified malaria (Malaria)
    - [I10]     Essential (primary) hypertension (Hypertension)
    - [E11]     Type 2 diabetes mellitus (Type 2 Diabetes)
    - [A15.0]   Tuberculosis of lung (Pulmonary Tuberculosis)
    - [J45.909] Unspecified asthma, uncomplicated (Bronchial Asthma)
    - [N39.0]   Urinary tract infection, site not specified (UTI)
    - [A90]     Dengue fever, unspecified (Dengue)
    - [E03.9]   Hypothyroidism, unspecified (Hypothyroidism)
    - [E05.9]   Thyrotoxicosis, unspecified (Hyperthyroidism)

*   DIAGNOSTIC TESTS (Mock Codes)
    - [TEST-MAL-RDT]    Malaria Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT)
    - [TEST-MAL-SMEAR]  Malaria Blood Smear
    - [TEST-BP-CHECK]   Blood Pressure Monitoring
    - [TEST-GLU-FPG]    Fasting Plasma Glucose (FPG)
    - [TEST-GLU-HBA1C]  Glycated Hemoglobin (HbA1c)
    - [TEST-TB-AFB]     Sputum Acid-Fast Bacilli (AFB) Smear
    - [TEST-TB-GENEX]   GeneXpert MTB/RIF Assay
    - [TEST-CBC]        Complete Blood Count (CBC)
    - [TEST-SPIROMETRY] Spirometry Lung Function Test
    - [TEST-URINALYSIS] Urinalysis (Dipstick/Microscopic)
    - [TEST-URINE-CUL]  Urine Culture & Sensitivity
    - [TEST-DEN-NS1]    Dengue NS1 Antigen Test
    - [TEST-DEN-IGM]    Dengue IgM Antibody Test
    - [TEST-THY-TSH]    Thyroid Stimulating Hormone (TSH) Test
    - [TEST-THY-FT4]    Free Thyroxine (FT4) Test

*   TREATMENTS & PROCEDURES (Mock CPT / RxNorm Codes)
    - [MED-AL-ACT]      Artemether-Lumefantrine (ACT) (Anti-malarial)
    - [MED-AML-5]       Amlodipine 5mg (Anti-hypertensive)
    - [MED-LIS-10]      Lisinopril 10mg (Anti-hypertensive)
    - [MED-MET-500]     Metformin 500mg (Anti-diabetic)
    - [MED-INS-GLAR]    Insulin Glargine (Anti-diabetic)
    - [MED-TB-HRZE]     Rifampicin/Isoniazid/Pyrazinamide/Ethambutol (Anti-TB)
    - [MED-SAL-INH]     Salbutamol Inhaler (Bronchodilator)
    - [MED-FLU-INH]     Fluticasone Inhaler (Inhaled Corticosteroid)
    - [MED-NIT-100]     Nitrofurantoin 100mg (Antibiotic)
    - [MED-CIP-500]     Ciprofloxacin 500mg (Antibiotic)
    - [MED-PAR-500]     Paracetamol 500mg (Analgesic/Antipyretic)
    - [MED-IBU-400]     Ibuprofen 400mg (NSAID - Contraindicated for Dengue)
    - [MED-LEV-50]      Levothyroxine Sodium 50mcg (Thyroid Replacement)
    - [MED-LEV-100]     Levothyroxine Sodium 100mcg (Thyroid Replacement)
    - [MED-CARB-10]     Carbimazole 10mg (Antithyroid)
    - [MED-METH-5]      Methimazole 5mg (Antithyroid)
    - [MED-PROP-40]     Propranolol 40mg (Beta-Blocker)

2. STANDARD TREATMENT PROTOCOL (STP) RULES
------------------------------------------------------------------------

Each claim's chronological `carePathway` must follow these rules:

---

### PROTOCOL 1: Malaria Uncomplicated Protocol
*   **Target Diagnosis**: B54 (Malaria)
*   **Rule 1.1: Diagnostic Before Treatment (Mandatory)**
    - *Condition*: Prescribing `MED-AL-ACT` requires a prior diagnostic test 
      (`TEST-MAL-RDT` or `TEST-MAL-SMEAR`) with a positive result.
    - *Deviation*: Missing diagnostic confirmation before anti-malarial.
    - *Severity*: CRITICAL (Penalty: 40 points)
*   **Rule 1.2: Therapy Alignment**
    - *Condition*: Primary diagnosis `B54` must list `MED-AL-ACT` as the primary 
      prescribed medication.
    - *Deviation*: Non-approved therapeutic for Malaria.
    - *Severity*: MAJOR (Penalty: 20 points)

---

### PROTOCOL 2: Essential Hypertension Protocol
*   **Target Diagnosis**: I10 (Hypertension)
*   **Rule 2.1: BP Check Confirmation (Mandatory)**
    - *Condition*: Claim must contain a `TEST-BP-CHECK` step showing elevated readings.
    - *Deviation*: Prescribing anti-hypertensive without BP record.
    - *Severity*: CRITICAL (Penalty: 40 points)
*   **Rule 2.2: First-Line Monotherapy**
    - *Condition*: Meds must be limited to `MED-AML-5` or `MED-LIS-10`.
    - *Deviation*: Non-approved medication for hypertension.
    - *Severity*: MAJOR (Penalty: 20 points)

---

### PROTOCOL 3: Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus Protocol
*   **Target Diagnosis**: E11 (Type 2 Diabetes)
*   **Rule 3.1: Glycemic Testing (Mandatory)**
    - *Condition*: Prescribing `MED-MET-500` or `MED-INS-GLAR` requires a prior 
      `TEST-GLU-FPG` or `TEST-GLU-HBA1C` record.
    - *Deviation*: Missing glycemic diagnostic verification.
    - *Severity*: CRITICAL (Penalty: 40 points)
*   **Rule 3.2: Line of Treatment Sequencing**
    - *Condition*: Insulin (`MED-INS-GLAR`) should only be prescribed if there is 
      a documented Metformin (`MED-MET-500`) trial or HbA1c result > 10%.
    - *Deviation*: Metformin trial bypassed without clinical justification.
    - *Severity*: MAJOR (Penalty: 25 points)

---

### PROTOCOL 4: Pulmonary Tuberculosis Protocol
*   **Target Diagnosis**: A15.0 (Pulmonary Tuberculosis)
*   **Rule 4.1: Microbiological Confirmation (Mandatory)**
    - *Condition*: Prescribing anti-TB therapy (`MED-TB-HRZE`) requires positive 
      `TEST-TB-AFB` (Sputum smear) or `TEST-TB-GENEX` (GeneXpert) results.
    - *Deviation*: TB therapy initiated without lab confirmation.
    - *Severity*: CRITICAL (Penalty: 40 points)
*   **Rule 4.2: Multi-Drug Regimen Adherence**
    - *Condition*: Prescription must consist of combined `MED-TB-HRZE` formulation. 
      Single-drug treatment is contraindicated for active TB.
    - *Deviation*: Sub-standard monotherapy regimen for active tuberculosis.
    - *Severity*: CRITICAL (Penalty: 40 points)

---

### PROTOCOL 5: Bronchial Asthma Protocol
*   **Target Diagnosis**: J45.909 (Bronchial Asthma)
*   **Rule 5.1: Airflow Assessment**
    - *Condition*: First-time asthma diagnosis and prescription (`MED-SAL-INH` 
      or `MED-FLU-INH`) requires a `TEST-SPIROMETRY` or peak expiratory flow test.
    - *Deviation*: Asthma therapy started without baseline lung function test.
    - *Severity*: MAJOR (Penalty: 20 points)
*   **Rule 5.2: Controller-Reliever Logic**
    - *Condition*: Chronic/daily therapy must include controller inhaler 
      (`MED-FLU-INH`) alongside rescue inhaler (`MED-SAL-INH`).
    - *Deviation*: Reliever inhaler monotherapy for persistent asthma.
    - *Severity*: MAJOR (Penalty: 20 points)

---

### PROTOCOL 6: Urinary Tract Infection (UTI) Protocol
*   **Target Diagnosis**: N39.0 (UTI)
*   **Rule 6.1: Lab Verification (Mandatory)**
    - *Condition*: Prescribing `MED-NIT-100` or `MED-CIP-500` requires a prior 
      positive test (`TEST-URINALYSIS` or `TEST-URINE-CUL`).
    - *Deviation*: Antibiotics prescribed for UTI without lab confirmation.
    - *Severity*: CRITICAL (Penalty: 35 points)
*   **Rule 6.2: First-line Selection**
    - *Condition*: Billed antibiotic must be `MED-NIT-100`. Broad-spectrum 
      `MED-CIP-500` should only be billed if there is a documented urine culture 
      indicating resistance or pyelonephritis.
    - *Deviation*: Broad-spectrum fluoroquinolone used without resistance evidence.
    - *Severity*: MAJOR (Penalty: 20 points)

---

### PROTOCOL 7: Dengue Fever Protocol
*   **Target Diagnosis**: A90 (Dengue)
*   **Rule 7.1: Confirmatory Test (Mandatory)**
    - *Condition*: Dengue diagnosis or treatment requires prior positive 
      `TEST-DEN-NS1` or `TEST-DEN-IGM` record.
    - *Deviation*: Dengue claim submitted without confirmatory laboratory testing.
    - *Severity*: CRITICAL (Penalty: 35 points)
*   **Rule 7.2: NSAID Contraindication (Stewardship)**
    - *Condition*: Claim must not prescribe NSAIDs like Ibuprofen (`MED-IBU-400`) 
      or Aspirin due to risk of hemorrhagic complications.
    - *Deviation*: Prescribed contraindicated NSAID for Dengue, increasing hemorrhage risk.
    - *Severity*: CRITICAL (Penalty: 50 points)
*   **Rule 7.3: Platelet Monitoring (Recommended)**
    - *Condition*: Dengue claim must include a Complete Blood Count (`TEST-CBC`) 
      to monitor platelet count and hematocrit.
    - *Deviation*: Missing platelet count/hematocrit monitoring for Dengue.
    - *Severity*: MAJOR (Penalty: 25 points)

---

### PROTOCOL 8: Hypothyroidism Protocol
*   **Target Diagnosis**: E03.9 (Hypothyroidism)
*   **Rule 8.1: Thyroid Function Testing (Mandatory)**
    - *Condition*: Prescribing Levothyroxine (`MED-LEV-50` or `MED-LEV-100`) 
      requires a prior elevated `TEST-THY-TSH` record.
    - *Deviation*: Thyroid hormone replacement initiated without TSH verification.
    - *Severity*: CRITICAL (Penalty: 40 points)
*   **Rule 8.2: Approved Therapy Alignment**
    - *Condition*: Billed medication must be Levothyroxine (`MED-LEV-50` or `MED-LEV-100`).
    - *Deviation*: Non-approved medication for hypothyroidism.
    - *Severity*: MAJOR (Penalty: 20 points)

---

### PROTOCOL 9: Hyperthyroidism Protocol
*   **Target Diagnosis**: E05.9 (Hyperthyroidism)
*   **Rule 9.1: Thyroid Function Testing (Mandatory)**
    - *Condition*: Prescribing Carbimazole (`MED-CARB-10`) or Methimazole (`MED-METH-5`) 
      requires a prior suppressed `TEST-THY-TSH` record.
    - *Deviation*: Antithyroid therapy initiated without TSH verification.
    - *Severity*: CRITICAL (Penalty: 40 points)
*   **Rule 9.2: First-Line Antithyroid Choice**
    - *Condition*: Billed antithyroid drug must be Carbimazole (`MED-CARB-10`) 
      or Methimazole (`MED-METH-5`).
    - *Deviation*: Non-approved antithyroid medication used as first-line therapy.
    - *Severity*: MAJOR (Penalty: 20 points)

3. SCORING ENGINE LOGIC (For Developer 2)
------------------------------------------------------------------------

*   **Initial Legitimacy Score**: 100 points.
*   **Score Deductions**: Deduct points dynamically based on the penalty value
    associated with each triggered deviation.
*   **Risk Categorization Rules**:
    - **Score 80 - 100**: LOW RISK (Priority 5) -> Green Flag. Auto-approvable.
    - **Score 50 - 79**: MEDIUM RISK (Priority 3) -> Yellow Flag. Needs reviewer eyes.
    - **Score 0 - 49**: HIGH RISK (Priority 1) -> Red Flag. Sent to top of reviewer queue.
